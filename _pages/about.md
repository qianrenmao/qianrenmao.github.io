[![Google Scholar](https://img.shields.io/badge/Google%20Scholar-blue?logo=google-scholar)](https://scholar.google.com)

I am currently working at Zhongguancun Laboratory (ZGCLAB), leading a research team focused on data security and large language models. If you are interested in academic collaboration, please feel free to contact me at maoqr@zgclab.edu.com. We are hiring interns.

I received my Ph.D. from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University ([SCSE, Beihang University](https://scse.buaa.edu.cn/index.html)), under the supervision of Professor [Jianxin Li](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=EY2lqD0AAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=ao). His Google Scholar profile shows the latest citation count.

I completed both my Bachelor's and Master's degrees at Xihua Normal University and was honored as an Excellent Graduate of Sichuan Province.

I also collaborate closely with Professor [Chenghua Lin](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=Tp1RdIQAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=ao) from the University of Manchester, and I have a long-term collaboration with Distinguished Professor [Philip S. Yu](https://cs.uic.edu/profiles/philip-yu/) from the University of Illinois Chicago. His Google Scholar profile provides the latest citation count.

My research interests include speech synthesis, neural machine translation, and automatic music generation. I have published more than 50 papers at top international AI conferences such as NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, and KDD.

You can also find my publications and profile on [ACL Anthology](https://aclanthology.org/people/qianren-mao/).

To support communication within the Chinese ML and NLP community, I co-founded the MLNLP community in 2021 with 11 other young scholars worldwide, and I am honored to serve as a member of the MLNLP committee.

For the most up-to-date citation counts, please visit my Google Scholar profile.
